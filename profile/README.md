## Hi there 👋 We are Green Circle

We are an environmental Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) dedicated to urban forestry, ecological conservation, and climate action through technological innovation.

---

### 🙋‍♀️ About Our Organization
Green Circle focuses on restoring and protecting biodiversity. Our flagship initiative is the **Tree Geo-Tagging & Digitalization Project**. This is a proprietary project managed by our internal technical team. Using spatial data mapping tools, mobile tech, and dynamic QR codes, we catalog local tree species, monitor their health, and calculate carbon sequestration data to create sustainable urban ecosystems.

---

### 🌈 Internal Team Collaboration
This repository is restricted to authorized Green Circle team members and assigned engineers. Our internal roadmap focuses on:
*   **Mobile Field App:** Optimizing GPS coordination and offline syncing capabilities for field drives.
*   **Data Pipelines:** Scaling our backend to handle high-frequency GIS updates from multiple campuses.
*   **Security & Accuracy:** Ensuring verified data entry and secure access to our centralized tree inventory.

👉 Team members should read the internal `ONBOARDING.md` guide to set up their restricted API keys and local development configurations.
